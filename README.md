Ecommerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of e-commerce sales performance, enabling businesses to monitor revenue, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and payment trends.

Key Insights
Total Sales Amount: ₹438K
Total Profit: ₹37K
Total Quantity Sold: 6K units
Average Order Value (AOV): ₹121K

Dashboard Features
Quarter-wise Filtering: Analyze performance across different quarters using interactive slicers.
Profit by Month: Track monthly profit trends and identify high-performing and loss-making periods.
Top 5 States by Profit: Compare profitability across different states.

Category-wise Quantity Analysis: Visualize sales distribution among Clothing, Electronics, and Furniture categories.

Top 5 Customers: Identify customers contributing the highest profit.

Payment Mode Analysis: Understand customer payment preferences (COD, UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, EMI).

Top 5 Sub-Categories by Profit: Highlight the most profitable product sub-categories.
